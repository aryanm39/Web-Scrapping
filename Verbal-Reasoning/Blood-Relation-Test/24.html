Which of the following means that K is the mother-in-law of M?
M9N3K4J
M9N5K3J
K5J9M3N
K3J9N4M
Answer: Option
Explanation:

M9N → M is the husband of N

N5K → N is the daughter of K

Hence, → M is the son-in-law of K

K3J → K is the mother of J

Hence, → K is a lady

Hence, → K is the mother-in-law of M.
