A train speeds past a pole in 15 seconds and a platform 100 m long in 25 seconds. Its length is:
50 m
150 m
200 m
Data inadequate
Answer: Option
Explanation:

Let the length of the train be x metres and its speed by y m/sec.

Then, x = 15         y = x .
y 15

x + 100 = x
25 15

15(x + 100) = 25x

15x + 1500 = 25x

1500 = 10x

x = 150 m.
