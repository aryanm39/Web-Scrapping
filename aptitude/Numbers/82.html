The sum all even natural numbers between 1 and 31 is:
16
128
240
512
Answer: Option
Explanation:

Required sum = (2 + 4 + 6 + ... + 30)

This is an A.P. in which a = 2, d = (4 - 2) = 2 and l = 30.

Let the number of terms be n. Then,

tn = 30    a + (n - 1)d = 30

2 + (n - 1) x 2 = 30

n - 1 = 14

n = 15

Sn = n (a + l) = 15 x (2 + 30)   = 240.
